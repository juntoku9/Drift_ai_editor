ATLAS TRAVEL COPILOT
Product Requirements Document
Owners: Product and Engineering
Updated by: Alina Zhou, Legal Counsel
Status: Working draft

Product Goal
Atlas provides informational travel planning suggestions and editable day plans.

Launch Plan in Current Draft
Q2 gated beta remains plan.
No fixed public date may be communicated before legal and privacy sign-off.

Scope in Current Draft
One-prompt itinerary generation.
Hotel and activity recommendations.
Editable day-by-day planner.
Shareable itinerary links.
Confidence cues and limitation disclosures included.
Terms acknowledgment required before first itinerary generation.
Profile deletion workflow required.
Direct booking links remain deferred.

Mandatory Legal and Privacy Requirements
User-facing language must state recommendations are informational and not guaranteed availability, pricing, or safety advice.
Terms acceptance must occur before first itinerary request.
Passport and government-ID data collection is prohibited in MVP.
Recommendation generation and acknowledgment events must be auditable.

Performance and Reliability Expectations
Reliability gates from engineering revision remain in force.
p95 beta target remains under 15 seconds.
Blank-itinerary failure threshold remains below 2 percent for two consecutive weeks.

Success Metrics
Completion and retention targets retained.
Policy-violation tracking added as launch health metric.

Risks
Legal non-compliance risk now equals reliability risk in launch decisioning.
Paid placement concepts are blocked pending disclosure framework.

Open Questions
Whether geo-specific disclaimer variants are required.
Whether under-18 access should be restricted in initial release.
